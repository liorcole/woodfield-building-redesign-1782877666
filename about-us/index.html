About Woodfield

A family run building firm with decades of site memory.

Established in 1988 by founder Arthur McArdle, Woodfield Building Services has built a team of qualified craftsmen working across small and large projects.

Established in 1988 by founder Arthur McArdle, who has many years of experience in the construction industry, Woodfield Building Services have built a team of craftsmen fully qualified in their particular trade.

The team has worked on both small and large projects that have been completed to the highest standard.

Based in Cannock, Staffordshire, Woodfield Building Services provide a professional service to individuals and businesses alike, offering insurance backed guarantees to all clients no matter how big or small the project.
